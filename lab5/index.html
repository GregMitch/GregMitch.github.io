Hair slashing since 1473

Hair Slashers find its roots all the way back to 1473 when our founder Aric Doofensmirtz started using swords to slash hair. While he was training to be a blacksmith he knew life at the forge was not for him as he wanted to use the swords he was creating. From that year forward Aric dedicated his craft to slashing hair and passed his practices down through generations to yours truely!!!
